03PRIORITY 1Opening (Turns 1–3)

Goal: stable, connected, defensible engine. Messed-up openings lose more Cat games than anything else.

Turn 1 — Ignition

Default
Build → Overwork → Build
  1. Sawmill (safe clearing)
  2. Overwork (if matching card)
  3. Recruiter (chokepoint)
No Overwork card
Build → Build → (March?)
  • 2 builds > anything else
  • March only: secure 3-slot / wood / block Alliance
Placement Sawmill = safe clearing · Recruiter = chokepoint · prefer 2–3 slot clearings.

Turn 2 — Stabilize vs Scale

Safe — Greedy
Sawmill → Overwork → Sawmill
Contested — Stable
Recruiter → Recruit → Sawmill
Strong Craft — Workshop
Only if you can craft Cobbler / Tax Collector / Command Post this turn.

Turn 3 — Engine Online

Default Recruit → Build → Build
Disrupted Battle → Build → Recruit
High tempo Bird card = extra action = 2–3 builds

07PRIORITY 2Combat — Don't Fight

🚫 Do NOT fight by default.

Beginners lose by fighting too much. Each wasted battle = 1 lost build = real VP.

Fight ONLY if…

  1. Enables a build (clears a slot)
  2. Restores rule (reconnects wood)
  3. High-value target (Alliance sympathy, exposed buildings)
Never
  • Just to "slow" an opponent
  • Without immediate gain
  • "Because you have warriors left"
ESSENTIAL

06PRIORITY 3Movement — Don't March

🚫 Do NOT March by default.

Counter-intuitive: Cats maintain connectivity, not expansion. Marching without reason = wasted action.

March ONLY if…

  1. Secures a 3-slot clearing
  2. Connects wood supply lines
  3. Prevents Alliance revolts
  4. Blocks opponent expansion
Phases Early: minimal · Mid: controlled · Late: only for last-build setup.

  • Enables future builds
  • Prevents engine collapse
Bad Action

  • Moving without purpose
  • Crafting without impact

15Matchups (All 8 Factions)

Eyrie
Threat: decree, turmoil inevitable.
Counter: race before decree explodes · build dense · let turmoil work.
Fight: roost blocking wood path.
Woodland Alliance
Threat: sympathy → revolts destroy buildings.
Counter: kill sympathy immediately · ≤1 building per sympathized clearing · don't discard birds to them.
Fight: before 3+ supporters.
Vagabond
Threat: mobile, crafts, quests, late ally.
Counter: don't aid · no 1-warrior stacks · protect Keep.
Fight: Hostile + cheap kill.
Lizard Cult
Threat: gardens snowball · outlasts rulers.
Counter: kill gardens early · deny outcast suit · don't let snowball past T5.
Fight: every reachable garden.
Riverfolk
Threat: services + funds · trade posts lock clearings.
Counter: don't buy unless build-enabling · kill trade posts in wood zones · starve funds.
Fight: trade post on sawmill clearing.
Corvid Conspiracy
Threat: plots disrupt · bombs wipe stacks.
Counter: expose plots near buildings · no stacks on plots · accept exposure cost.
Fight: plot adjacent to 2+ buildings.
Underground Duchy
Threat: burrows · crown scoring · ministers snowball.
Counter: race crown · deny ministerial suit · keep ministers swayable.
Fight: tunnels in your core.
Keepers in Iron
Threat: relic recovery · waystation VP chains.
Counter: block relic corridors · recover relics yourself · deny waystation chain.
Fight: waystation in a 3-slot you need.

16Map Openings

Autumn
Standard · defaults apply · Keep in SW corner · Sawmill in adjacent 2-slot rabbit · Recruiter in fox chokepoint · center most contested.
Winter
No suits on clearings · craft/Overwork from cards alone · hoard cards before workshop · bias Recruiter · delay workshop to T3.
Lake
Rivers + ferry skip lanes · harder to defend · sawmills not adjacent to rivers · stacks near landings.
Mountain
Passes restrict movement · seize a pass early · sawmills behind passes near-invulnerable · Field Hospitals value spikes.
